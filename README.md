A modern Progressive Web App (PWA) for tracking and managing your power naps.

## Description

Napflow is a user-friendly app that helps you plan, track, and optimize your power naps. With a gamified XP system, streak tracking, and detailed statistics, Napflow makes napping a motivating experience.

## Features

- **Smart Nap Timer** with predefined presets (Power Focus, Quick Refresh, Deep Recharge) or custom time
- **XP & Level System** - Earn XP for each nap and progress through 5 levels
- **Streak Tracking** - Maintain your daily nap streak
- **Weekly Statistics** - Visualize your naps with an interactive chart
- **Notifications & Alarm** - Get notified when your nap is over
- **Multi-language** - Supports German and English
- **Cloud Sync** - All data is securely stored in the cloud
- **PWA** - Installable on iOS and Android, works offline

## Tech Stack

- React 19.2.1
- Vite 7.2.7
- Tailwind CSS
- Supabase (Backend & Authentication)
